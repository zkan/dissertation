\begin{center}
{\bf Abstract \\ \vskip 1em}
\vskip 1em
\end{center}
\singlespace
\doublespace
\hspace{8.5mm}
\vspace{-1em}

Much of the traffic accidents that occur on the roads are due to human cause. By 
detecting road lanes systems can be developed for providing drivers assistance 
to the driver or making sure that traffic laws are being enforced. This thesis 
proposes techniques for detecting road lanes under two scenarios. The first is 
by placing a camera in front of the car and detecting the lanes as driving. 
Distances from the lane can be calculated which is a useful information for 
systems like lane departure warning. The second is a camera placed at the side 
of the road that detects traffic violations such as detecting vehicle overtaking 
at steep curves.
